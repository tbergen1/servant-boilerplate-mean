"The Servant Screen"

Here you can choose which Servant you would like to use with this application.
Each Servant gets its own text messaging account with a dedicated phone number to send and receive text messages from.
Tiny Texts stored on the selected Servant are used as the body of the text messages. Subscribers to text message marketing campaigns are kept on the selected Servant's Contacts with the tag 'text-marketing-active'.
You can return here at any time by clicking “Servants” at the top to choose a different Servant.
